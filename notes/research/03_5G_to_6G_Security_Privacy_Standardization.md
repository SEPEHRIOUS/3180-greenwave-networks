# Paper Summary

**Full Citation:** From 5G to 6G: A Survey on Security, Privacy, and Standardization Pathways. https://arxiv.org/html/2410.21986v1

## Summary
This survey traces the evolution from 5G to 6G specifically through the lens of 
security, privacy, and standardization. It reviews prior surveys (including 
Nguyen et al. above), and focuses on what still needs to be standardized — for 
example around AI-driven network functions — rather than just cataloguing 
technical threats.

## Relevance to Our Survey
This is useful as a bridge between the purely technical privacy risks (Nguyen) 
and the governance/standardization angle, since standardization bodies like 
3GPP are directly relevant to my Proposed Future Approach section on tiered 
consent models.

## Critical Comment
Standardization-focused papers can sometimes be more aspirational than 
concrete — worth checking whether it proposes actual standard specifications or 
just argues standardization is needed.
