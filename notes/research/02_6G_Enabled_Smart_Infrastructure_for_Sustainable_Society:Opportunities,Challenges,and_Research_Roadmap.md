3. Abstract:<br>
This paper introduce the facilitate 6G and introduce emerging 6G applications; it also discuss the technology-driven challenges, social, psychological, health and commercialization issues posed to actualizing 6G, and the probable solutions to tackle these challenges are discussed extensively.  Finally, it highlight the research trends, open research issues, and key take-away lessons for future research exploration in 6G wireless communication.

## Relevance to Our Survey:

3. Detail explaination of relevance:<br>
This paper is highly relevant to our issue because it provides a new perspective on what 6G can achieve. It discusses the development potential of 6G in the Internet of Bio-Nano Things, Digital Replica, Industry 4.0, and beyond. It also explores customer trust issues arising from users' potential health concerns about electromagnetic radiation, as well as sustainability concerns related to business capabilities and maintenance challenges.
