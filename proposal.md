## Selected Research Topic

**Topic 8 — The Sustainability Paradox: AI for Green Networks vs. Energy-Hungry AI**

## Topic Justification

Artificial intelligence will play a major role in the future of 6G wireless networks by automating network management, 
improving energy efficiency, and reducing unnecessary infrastructure usage. However, large-scale AI systems also require significant computational power, 
electricity, and cooling resources, creating a sustainability contradiction. This topic is important for understanding the future of telecommunications, 
where balancing large AI models with energy optimization is critical. Our project will examine the current state of 6G infrastructure, 
AI-assisted energy optimization, and the environmental impact of AI systems. The primary goal is to identify existing research addressing 
this tradeoff and to evaluate approaches that balance energy consumption, network performance, and the broader sustainability 
responsibilities of future wireless networks.
